When to Use This Checklist

Use this checklist if you suspect fraud occurred in your Texas custody case, such as:

  • A false Certificate of Last Known Address was filed
  • You were served at an address where you did not live
  • Service by publication was used when the petitioner knew your address
  • A default judgment was entered without your knowledge
  • An attorney made false statements to the court

This evidence may be used in a Bill of Review, State Bar grievance, criminal complaint, or legislative testimony.
