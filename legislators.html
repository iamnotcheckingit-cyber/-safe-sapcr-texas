What to Say When You Call

Sample Script

"Hello, my name is [YOUR NAME] and I'm a constituent from [YOUR CITY]. I'm calling to ask [REPRESENTATIVE/SENATOR NAME] to support family law reform legislation that would require due diligence before attorneys can certify a respondent's address in custody cases. Currently, weak verification requirements enable service fraud and wrongful default judgments that separate families. Please support legislation that protects parents' due process rights. Thank you."

Every call matters. Legislators track constituent contacts and prioritize issues their voters care about.
