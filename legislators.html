What to Say When You Call

Sample Script

"Hello, my name is [YOUR NAME] and I'm a constituent from [YOUR CITY]. I'm calling to ask [REPRESENTATIVE/SENATOR NAME] to support the SAFE SAPCR Act, which would require due diligence before attorneys can certify a respondent's address in family court custody cases. Currently, Texas Rule 239a has no verification requirement, which enables service fraud and wrongful default judgments that separate Texas families. Please support legislation that protects parents' due process rights. Thank you."

Every call matters. Legislators track constituent contacts and prioritize issues their voters care about.
